A Breif History of DR. A.P.J Abdul Kalam

  • 1931 - Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was born on 15 October 1931 to a Tamil Muslim family in rameswaram
  • 1954 - Graduated from Saint Joseph's College, Tiruchirappali
  • 1955 - Enrolled at MIT to study Aerospace Engineering
  • 1960 - Joined DRDO as Cheif Scientist
  • 1965 - Started to work on Rocket Project independently
  • 1969 - Got Transfferd to ISRO
  • 1980-90 - Responsible for the development of AGNI and PRITHIVI Missiles
  • 1981 - Got Padma Bhusan award from goverment of india
  • 1990 - Got Padma Vibhusan award from goverment of india
  • 1992-99 - Served as the Cheif Scientific Adviser to th ePM and the Secretary of DRDO
  • 1997 - got Bharat Ratna From Government of India
  • 2002-07 - Served as the 11th President of India
  • 2012 - Launched a Mission for the youth of the nation called "What Can I Give Movement"
  • 2015 - Passed away on 27 July in Shilong Meghalaya
